Getting Started with Ethical Hacking

Ethical hacking is a rewarding career path for those interested in cybersecurity. Here's how to begin:

  • Learn networking fundamentals
  • Study operating systems (especially Linux)
  • Understand web technologies
  • Practice with platforms like TryHackMe and Hack The Box
  • Get certified (CEH, OSCP, etc.)

Remember: Ethical hacking requires permission and should always be done legally and responsibly.
